# COPY REVIEW REPORT: Johanna Skincare Website

**Reviewed by**: Copy Review Skill (PASTOR Framework, adapted for personal brand skincare)
**Date**: 2026-02-20
**Target audience**: Colombian women, 25-55, with skin problems (manchas, acne, piel grasa, envejecimiento)
**Site**: https://johanna-skincare.vercel.app

---

## PAGE 1: HOMEPAGE

### PASTOR Framework Score

```
Problem:    3/10 - Pain point implied but never stated directly
Agitate:    2/10 - No agitation whatsoever
Solution:   6/10 - Hybrid method presented but as concept, not solution to pain
Testimony:  5/10 - Two testimonials exist but lack depth
Offer:      4/10 - Offer is buried; free consultation not in hero
Response:   5/10 - CTAs exist but miss emotional triggers
```

**Overall PASTOR Score: 4.2/10**

### Critical Issues

1. **Hero headline** describes METHOD, not OUTCOME. Woman with manchas doesn't care about "botica natural vs alta tecnologia"
2. **Hero CTAs** are brand-centric ("Linea Natural Shelo" / "Alta Cosmetica MK"), not benefit-centric
3. **"GRATIS" is ABSENT** from the entire homepage hero - the strongest offer is invisible
4. **Badge "Consultoria de Belleza Hibrida de Autor"** is insider jargon
5. **SmartRecommender sends to WRONG WhatsApp** (573124567890 instead of 573133747275)

### Suggested Hero Rewrite

```
Badge: "Asesoria GRATIS por WhatsApp - Envios a toda Colombia"
Headline: "Manchas, acne, piel apagada? Transformo tu piel en 15 dias"
Subheadline: "Soy Johanna, asesora con 10+ anos de experiencia. Te diseno una rutina personalizada GRATIS combinando lo mejor de Shelo Nabel y Mary Kay."
CTA Primary: "Quiero mi Asesoria GRATIS" (WhatsApp)
CTA Secondary: "Ver como funciona" (scroll)
```
